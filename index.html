Who we are

We pride ourselves on being a part of the community at ALKAIOS and we provide a fully equipped gym and fitness studios.
Our enthusiastic and fully qualified staff are always on hand to assist with your queries.
Our centres are the perfect place for a family day out, to keep fit, to meet new friends or just to relax and unwind.
The positive impact we are able to make in the community is extremely important to us.
The Outstanding Q Mark award recently gained from Ireland Active National Standard is a great source of pride.
All surplus profits made are reinvested back into the business for the benefit of all by introducing new equipment and programmes.
