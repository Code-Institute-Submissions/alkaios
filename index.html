What we Do

We pride ourselves on being a part of the community At ALKAIOS, we provide a fully equipped gym and fitness studios. Our enthusiastic and fully qualified staff are always on hand to assist with your queries.
Having recently achieved the Outstanding Award in the Ireland Active National Standard and Outstanding Q Mark award.
Our centres are the perfect place for a family day out, to keep fit, to meet new friends or just to relax and unwind.
The positive impact we are able to make in the community is extremely important to us.
Any surplus profits made are reinvested back into the business for the benefit of all by introducing new equipment and programmes.
